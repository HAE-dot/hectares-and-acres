What we offer

Every service tied to the ground you're working with.

From the first soil test to the final title deed, here's how Hectares & Acres Enterprise supports you.

01 — Agriculture

Farming Services

We support growers at every stage of the season, from land preparation to harvest planning.

  • Land clearing, tilling and soil assessment
  • Crop selection and rotation planning
  • Farm management and productivity advisory
  • Irrigation and equipment recommendations
02 — Real Estate

Land Sales

We help buyers and sellers move land with confidence, backed by proper valuation and paperwork.

  • Land sourcing for agricultural, residential and commercial use
  • Market valuation and pricing guidance
  • Sale negotiation and documentation support
  • Post-sale transfer assistance
03 — Surveying

Land Surveying & Consulting

Before you build, lease, or buy, we confirm exactly what you're dealing with.

  • Boundary surveys and beacon placement
  • Title verification and ownership checks
  • Site due diligence reports
  • Dispute and encroachment advisory
04 — Minerals

Gold Mining Services & Consultancy

For claim holders and small-scale operators, we advise from prospecting through to compliance.

  • Claim and site assessment
  • Exploration and extraction guidance
  • Licensing and regulatory support
  • Operational and safety advisory
05 — Design

Architectural Services

We design and draw the buildings that sit on the land — residential, commercial and hospitality spaces alike.

  • Site and block planning
  • Architectural and structural drawings
  • 3D visualization and photorealistic renders
  • Interior design for homes, restaurants and bars
